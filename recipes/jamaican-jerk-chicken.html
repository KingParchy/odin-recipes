Description

Jamaican Jerk Chicken is a world-renowned dish defined by its bold, fiery, and aromatic flavor profile. The process begins with a "jerk" marinade or dry rub, traditionally built on a foundation of crushed allspice berries (pimento) and Scotch bonnet peppers. These are blended with scallions, fresh thyme, ginger, garlic, and warm spices like cinnamon and nutmeg to create a complex balance of heat, sweetness, and earthiness.
